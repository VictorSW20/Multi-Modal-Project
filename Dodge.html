History of Dodge

Dodge, founded by brothers John and Horace Dodge in 1900, initially supplied parts and assemblies for Detroit's burgeoning automobile industry. By 1914, Dodge had transitioned to manufacturing complete vehicles, and during World War I, it played a crucial role in producing military vehicles. The brand gained prominence in the post-war era with models like the Dodge Power Wagon, which contributed to the growth of the pickup truck market. Over the decades, Dodge has become known for its muscle cars, with iconic models like the Dodge Charger and Challenger cementing its reputation for high-performance and bold design.

Dodge Viper

The Dodge Viper, introduced in 1992, is an American sports car known for its bold design, high-performance capabilities, and iconic V10 engine. With a powerful and distinctive 8.4-liter V10 powerplant, the Viper has been a symbol of raw American muscle, boasting impressive horsepower and torque figures. Over its production history, the Viper underwent various updates, showcasing Dodge's commitment to delivering a visceral driving experience and solidifying its status as a classic in the realm of high-performance sports cars.

Dodge Challenger

The Dodge Challenger, reintroduced in 2008, is a modern iteration of the classic American muscle car that first debuted in the early 1970s. Known for its bold and aggressive design, the Challenger pays homage to its heritage while incorporating contemporary performance and technology. With a range of powerful engines, including the iconic Hemi V8, the Dodge Challenger has become a symbol of raw power and nostalgia in the world of muscle cars.

Dodge Charger

The Dodge Charger, originally introduced in 1966, is an American muscle car that has evolved over the years into a modern, high-performance sedan. Known for its distinctive styling and powerful engines, the Charger has remained a symbol of muscle car heritage and bold design. The Charger's versatility, combining a spacious interior with potent performance options, has made it a popular choice for those seeking a blend of comfort and exhilarating driving dynamics.
